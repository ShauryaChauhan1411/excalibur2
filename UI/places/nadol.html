Nadol is a historic town located in Rajsamand district, Rajasthan, India, known for its ancient fort ruins and rich Rajput heritage. Once the stronghold of the Chauhan rulers of Nadol, it holds great historical significance as a strategic center during medieval Rajasthan. The town is surrounded by remnants of fortifications, old temples, and step wells that reflect the architectural style and defensive planning of its era. Notable sites include the ruins of the Nadol Fort and temples dedicated to various deities, which attract both history enthusiasts and pilgrims.
