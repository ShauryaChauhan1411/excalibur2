MEHRANGARH FORT

Explore the majestic heights of Mehrangarh Fort — one of India’s most awe-inspiring forts, towering 400 feet above the “Blue City” of Jodhpur, Rajasthan. Founded in 1459 by Rao Jodha, this colossal fort is a symbol of Rajput valor and royal grandeur. Its massive walls, some over 100 feet high, enclose palaces, courtyards, and temples, offering a breathtaking glimpse into centuries of history. Mehrangarh Fort is renowned for its exquisite architecture, intricate carvings, and beautiful palaces like the Sheesh Mahal, Jodhpur, Phool Mahal, and Moti Mahal. The fort’s museum showcases royal costumes, weapons, paintings, and artifacts from the glorious Marwar dynasty. From the ramparts, visitors can enjoy panoramic views of Jodhpur’s iconic blue houses spread across the city. Steeped in legends and stories of battles, Mehrangarh Fort is a must-visit destination for travelers who love history, culture, and spectacular architecture. Its grandeur leaves every visitor spellbound.
