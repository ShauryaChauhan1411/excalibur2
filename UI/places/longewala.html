LONGEWALA WAR MEMORIAL

Longewala War Memorial is a historic site located in Jaisalmer district, Rajasthan, India, commemorating the bravery of Indian soldiers during the Indo-Pak War of 1971. The memorial stands near the famous Longewala post, where a small contingent of Indian troops held off a much larger enemy force, showcasing remarkable courage and strategic defense. Visitors to the memorial can explore the battlefield site, see displays of tanks, weapons, and vehicles used during the battle, and read plaques detailing the heroic actions of the soldiers. The memorial also provides panoramic views of the surrounding desert, emphasizing the challenging conditions under which the battle was fought. A visit to Longewala War Memorial is not only a tribute to history and patriotism but also an educational experience for those interested in India’s military heritage. It is a must-visit for history enthusiasts, students, and travelers exploring Rajasthan’s desert forts and war landmarks.
