★4.5 HAWA MAHAL

Stunning example of Rajput artistry-the "Palace of Winds" with its 953 windows is breathtaking ,landmark symbolizing Jaipur’s royal past

★4.7 CHITTORGARH FORT

Symbolizes Rajput bravery, sacrifice, and honor, offers beautiful panormic views of the surrounding town and hills, massive fort complex with palaces, towers and temples each with intricate craving

★4.2 BHANGARH FORT

Mystique and legend, famously called India's most haunted fort, it's surrounded by eerie folklore and ghost stories, showcases classic Mughal-Rajput style stonework

★4.9 MEHRANGARH FORT

one of the best preserved and most majestic forts in india, massive courtyards, intricate carvings, and an exceptional museum showcasing royal artifacts, arms, and costumes

★4.8 KUMBALGARH FORT

It's the birthplace of the legendary Maharana pratap, massive and majestic surrounded by 36km long wall, offers stunning panormic views, especially at sunrise and during evening light shows

★4.6 JAISALMER FORT

One of living forts- people still reside, shop, and run cafes inside!, feels like walking through a golden medieval city, built from golden-yellow sandstone, beautifully craved Jain temples, countless rooftop restaurants with desert views

★4.3 JAL MAHAL

water palace, set in the middle of man sagar lake, a graceful blend of Rajput and Mughal styles, one of the most photographed landmark

★4.7 RANTHAMBORE NATIONAL PARK

Located in Sawai Madhopur,Originally as a game sanctuary in 1955, it became one of India’s first Project Tiger reserves in 1974, then declared a national park in 1980.eopards, sloth bears, jackals, nilgai, wild boars

★4.3 JANTAR MANTAR

It's a UNESCO World Heritage site built by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh ||, features precise astronomical instruments made of stone and metal , great for history and science lovers, unique astronomical instruments, educational and historical value

★3.8 NADOL

A small historic town known for its cultural heritage and ancient temples, limited tourist facilities, offbeat destination, beautiful temples and local history

★4.4 BUNDI

Bundi is a hidden gem of rajasthan, known for its magnificent forts, stepwells and blue-painted houses, rich in agriculture and heritage, peaceful and scenic old town charm

★4.7 LONGEWALA WAR
MEMORIAL

Longewala is a symbol of Indian bravery and patriotism, showcases tanks, bunkers, and real accounts from battlefields ,deeply patriotic and emotional experience

★4.6 SAM SAND DUNES

Offers the classic Thar dessert experience, golden dunes, camel safaris, jeep rides, and mesmerizing sunsets, breathtaking sunsets and sunrise views, thriling camel and jeep safaris

★4.5 MOUNT ABU

Rajasthan's only hill station, is a refreshing escape from the desert heat , surrounded by aravalli hills, offers scenic beauty, historic temples, and a pleasant climate year-round

★4.4 ASHAPURA MATA TEMPLE

The Ashapura Mata Temple in nadol is a reserved shrine dedicated to Goddess Ashapura, believed to fulfill devotees wishes, holds deep cultural and spiritual significance, especially among locals and Rajput communities

★4.7 AJMER SHARIF DARGAH

One of India's most scared and visited Sufi shrines, dedicated to Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti, a revered Sufi saint, attracts people of all faiths who come to seek blessings and peace, deeply spiritual and peaceful atmosphere

★4.8 SHRINATH JI TEMPLE

The Shrinathji Temple in Nathdwara is one of the most revered temples of Lord Krishna, depicting him as a seven-year-old child lifting the Govardhan Hill

★4.7 KHATU SHYAM JI TEMPLE

The Khatushyamji Temple is a famous pilgrimage site dedicated to Shyam Baba known for fulfilling devotees wishes, especially known for Phalgun Mela

★4.3 BRAHMA TEMPLE, PUSHKAR

The Brahma Temple in Pushkar is one of the very few temples in the world dedicated to lord Brahma, making it unique and historically significant, temple showcases traditional Rajasthani architecture

★4.6 EKLING JI TEMPLE

The Ekling Temple is a famous Shaivite temple complex near Udaipur, dedicated to Lord Shiva, tutelary diety of the Mewar dynasty, with intricate carvings, traditional Rajasthani architecture

★4.2 SAMBHAR SALT LAKE

Sambhar Salt Lake is India's largest inland salt lake, known foe its vast expanse, salt production, and migratory birds like flamingos, offers unique landscape,especially during sunset
